An Examination of Emma W's Tea Website

By: Travis Wood

I think the style choices you made for your Tea site gave it a very clean look. The menu page reads well and everything is nicely spaced. The photo you used with the DND dice was a great addition, and the tea names were fitting. It inspired to me to try making a webpage base of something else. So I tried crafting this review in the art style of a particular video game(link at bottom).

My Favorite Parts

  • The font is abvsolutely amazing! Probably the best fantasy style I have seen. Very easy to read, but still really unique looking.
  • The 'M's and the looped 'OO's are cool. I have font envy.
  • Tasteful choice in background color. Suddle whitish/blue maybe?
  • The menu picture goes great with the font color. It also has neat transparent effect.
  • The link at the bottom for crediting the author is well placed, and made me realize I need to think of that in the future

Feedback

Having trouble coming up with any potential improvements. Even your html and css is formatted nicely. The only real change I would make, would be rounding the edges on your about picture. Clearly I am biased, but I think it's a quick way to improve presentation. Other than that very very minor thing, I thought everything was great, nice job :)
